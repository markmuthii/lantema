1. A new business is registered. ✓

2. An application is submitted. ✓
3. A bill for the inspection fee is generated (constant amount).
4. Business pays the inspection fee. 
5. Receipt is generated and shared.
6. Inspection is done.
7. Result of the inspection is updated.

8. 
a) If the inspection is a pass: 
9. A license bill is generated.
10. Business pays for the bill.
11. Receipt is generated and shared.
12. License is generated and shared.

b) If the inspection is a fail:
9. Business reapplies (go back to 2 above)

13. License expires.
14. Business reapplies (go back to 2 above)
